Overview

Purpose

This site is created for online visibility, that will make it easy for potential customers to find the company when searchng for rafting activities. It's created also for providing detailed information about the different rafting options offered by the company. And so on.

Audience

The target audience include adventure seekers, people who are looking for thrillin outdoor experiences and enjoy activities that involve adrenaline and excitement. Nature enthousiasts,People who appreciate the beauty of nature and seek activities that allow them to immerse themselves in natural landscapes, such as rivers and canyons. Tourists, Travelers visiting the area where the rafting company operates, including both domestic and international tourists looking for unique and memorable experiences during their vacations, etc
